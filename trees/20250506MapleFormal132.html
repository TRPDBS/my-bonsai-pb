Bonsai Tree Detail

Tree Name: Maple
Style: Formal Upright
Species: Acer palmatum (Japanese Maple)
Age: 45 years
Current Owner: Hana Yamada
Previous Owner: Unknown (donated from Tokyo Garden Society)
Origin: Kyoto, Japan
Height: 42 cm

Story

This bonsai maple was cultivated from a cutting in 1980 by a master gardener in Kyoto. It was shaped in the formal upright style over decades and featured in the National Bonsai Exhibition in 2015. Known for its vibrant autumn foliage, it has become a showcase specimen for traditional Japanese techniques.
